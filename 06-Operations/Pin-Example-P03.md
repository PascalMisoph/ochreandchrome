## Why prices are not baked into the image

Pendant prices change. Affiliate links go out of stock. A pin with `$249` printed on it dies the moment that pendant is delisted. The pin headline says *Under $300* — a *price ceiling*, not a specific tag — so the pin stays evergreen even when individual products in the destination article get swapped. All four shown are placeholders for the article's curated 7; the article carries the live pricing.

## Pre-flight checks before generating

## Use as reference

Future agents producing **product roundup pins** should:
1. Copy the §7.A Product Roundup variant prompt above.
2. Replace `{CATEGORY_LABEL}`, `{HEADLINE}` (use number + product + price-anchor format), `{SUBLINE}`, and the 4 product descriptions in Zone 4.
3. Keep the **left-aligned** Zone 2 + Zone 3 — this is the visual signature of a product roundup vs the centred trend / educational pin.
4. **Never bake prices onto the pendants** — keep prices on the destination article only.
5. Always render 4 visibly distinct products (not four copies) — a curated edit, not a catalog row.
