## ChatGPT Images v2 prompt (fully-composed pin, §7.A)

> A complete, publication-ready editorial Pinterest pin design for the brand **Ochre & Chrome**. Vertical 2:3 aspect ratio, 1000 by 1500 pixels, premium interior-design-magazine feeling, mobile-first text hierarchy, fully composed end-to-end — no further editing required.
>
> **Layout — render exactly these five zones, top to bottom:**
>
> **Zone 1 — Top category label (≈8% of height).** A small uppercase tracked label set in a clean modern sans (Montserrat-feeling), letter-spacing wide, weight regular, in deep navy `#1A2A3A` on a warm cream `#F7F5F0` background strip. Text, exactly: `NEO DECO  ·  TREND REPORT`. Centred horizontally.
>
> **Zone 2 — Editorial headline (≈18% of height).** A large editorial serif headline in Playfair Display feeling, weight bold, in deep navy `#1A2A3A` on warm cream, set across two lines, generous tracking, centred horizontally. Text, exactly: `The Neo Deco / Revival`. Render the type sharp, mobile-readable, and free of generative artefacts.
>
> **Zone 3 — Supporting subline (≈6% of height).** A short supporting line in clean modern sans (Lato-feeling), weight regular, **upright (never italic), no trailing period**, in soft black `#2C2C2C` on warm cream, centred. Text, exactly: `Modern Art Deco for 2026`.
>
> **Zone 4 — Hero interior image (≈55% of height).** A premium architectural-digest-style editorial photograph of a Neo Deco living room hero scene, filling the central majority of the frame. Composition: an emerald velvet tuxedo sofa centred in the lower-middle of the image zone, with a sculptural unlacquered solid brass pendant light hanging into the image zone from the upper edge, casting a focused warm pool of light onto a low red marble coffee table directly in front of the sofa. On the coffee table sits one fluted glass vessel — no other clutter. Behind the sofa, mounted on a deep walnut lacquered wall, a large fan-arch geometric mirror catches a soft secondary reflection. A warm cream wool rug grounds the floor. Camera at eye level, slight side angle, sharp focus on the velvet weave, brass patina, and red marble veining.
>
> *Materials:* dense contract-grade emerald velvet, unlacquered solid brass with subtle living patina, dramatic red marble with strong oxblood veining (Calacatta Viola or red Portoro), vertically fluted clear glass, dark walnut high-sheen lacquer, soft warm-cream wool pile.
>
> *Palette:* warm ivory walls visible at edges, deep emerald velvet as the dominant colour mass, warm brushed brass, oxblood-red marble veining, dark walnut lacquer, soft cream rug. No pastels, no cool greys, no cool chromes.
>
> *Lighting:* cinematic warm key light from camera left at golden-hour temperature, softly raking across materials, pooling on the red marble surface, controlled shadow climbing the right side of the lacquered wall. One dominant pool of light from the pendant; everything else falls into warm shadow.
>
> *Mood:* cinematic restraint, modern Art Deco glamour, boutique-hotel-lounge calm, editorial magazine feeling, hush.
>
> **Zone 5 — Branded footer banner (≈13% of height).** A solid warm-cream `#F7F5F0` banner across the full width at the bottom of the pin. Centred inside the banner, on two stacked lines: first line, the wordmark `Ochre & Chrome` in Playfair Display feeling, weight regular, in deep navy `#1A2A3A`. Second line, immediately below, the domain `ochreandchrome.com` in Montserrat feeling, all-caps, tracked-out, weight regular, smaller scale, in soft black `#2C2C2C`. Optional thin micro-divider rule between the two lines in brushed brass `#D4AF37`. Render this footer banner sharp, readable, and free of typographic artefacts.
>
> **Brand visual identity:** Palette `#F7F5F0` cream, `#1A2A3A` navy, `#2C2C2C` soft black, `#D4AF37` brushed brass as restrained accent. Typography: Playfair Display for headline + wordmark, Lato for subline, Montserrat all-caps tracked for label + domain. Generous negative space, mobile-readable at thumbnail.
>
> **Strict do-not-include:** no people; no logos other than the Ochre & Chrome wordmark in Zone 5; no watermarks other than `ochreandchrome.com` in Zone 5; no neon; no glitter; no cursive or script fonts; no 1980s gold-plate kitsch; no Vegas-Deco pastiche; no plastic shades pretending to be brass; no Canva-template feel; no startup illustration; no chaotic overlapping product collages; no embedded text other than the specified label, headline, subline, wordmark, and domain.
>
> **Output:** a finished, publishable Pinterest pin image — ready to upload directly with no further editing.

### What worked

- Material trifecta (emerald velvet + unlacquered brass + red marble veining) is textbook Neo Deco.
- Cinematic warm-key pool from the pendant onto the marble = correct lighting language.
- Walnut wall with sunburst fan inset is a beautifully on-brand model invention.
- Headline + label + footer all render sharp and mobile-readable.

### Tweaks to bundle if regenerating v2

1. **Bigger fan-arch mirror.** Spec said it should anchor the wall; model rendered a smaller circular mirror. Add: *"a large fan-arch mirror at least 60% of the sofa width, brushed brass frame, centred above the sofa back."*
2. **More sculptural pendant.** Currently a flat-disc shape. Add: *"a multi-tier sculptural brass pendant with a fluted glass diffuser."*
3. **Visible rug.** Add: *"a warm cream high-pile wool rug visible across the front quarter of the floor."*
4. **No side tables.** Add: *"no side tables in frame; the room reads as restrained, single-focal."*
