# KPIs & Tracking System

## Weekly KPI Dashboard

| Metric | What it tells you |
|---|---|
| **Pinterest Impressions** | Top-of-funnel reach |
| **Saves** | Algorithm health (high saves = high quality) |
| **Outbound Clicks** | 🎯 *Most critical Pinterest metric* — traffic to website |
| **Pin CTR** | (Outbound Clicks / Impressions). Goal: > 1.0% |
| **Website Sessions** | Total visitors |
| **Affiliate Link Clicks** | Who actually clicked "Check Price" |
| **Affiliate Sales / Revenue** | Bottom line |
| **RPM** | Total Revenue / (Impressions / 1,000) |

---

## Master Spreadsheet Structure

### Tab 1 — Content Calendar
`Date | Blog Title | URL | Primary Keyword | Published? | Internal Links Added?`

### Tab 2 — Pinterest Pins
`Date Scheduled | Pin Title | Target URL | Board | Pin Format | 30-Day Outbound Clicks | CTR`

### Tab 3 — Affiliate Links
`Product Name | Retailer | Raw Link | Cloaked Link | Commission Rate | Clicks | Sales`

---

## Weekly Review Process — Fridays @ 4 PM

1. **Check Pinterest Analytics** — sort pins by Outbound Clicks (last 7 days). Identify top 3.
2. **Analyze winners** — what do the top 3 share? (Color · headline formula · topic?)
3. **Duplicate success** — instruct Canva operator to make 5 new variations of each top pin.
4. **Check affiliate dashboards** — which products have clicks but ZERO sales? Swap immediately for cheaper alternatives or better-reviewed products.
5. **Update links** — check for "Out of Stock" / 404 errors on top 5 traffic-driving posts.

---

## 30 / 60 / 90-Day Benchmarks

### Day 30
- ✅ **Minimum:** 10,000 impressions, 50 outbound clicks
- 🚩 **Red flag:** Zero outbound clicks → pins are pretty but lack CTA / curiosity hooks. Rework headlines using [[Headline-Formulas]].

### Day 60
- ✅ **Good:** 50,000 impressions, 500 outbound clicks, first 1–3 affiliate sales
- 🔄 **Pivot criteria:** Traffic high but affiliate clicks = 0 → blog post formatting is bad. Redesign CTA buttons larger/contrasting (Ochre on Cream).

### Day 90
- ✅ **Excellent:** 150,000+ impressions, 1,500+ outbound clicks, consistent daily revenue
- 🚀 **Double-down criteria:** Identify which aesthetic (Neo Deco vs Afrohemian) generated 80% of revenue. Shift 80% of month-4 production to that aesthetic.
