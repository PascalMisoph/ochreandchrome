When to keep, expand, rename, or create a Pinterest board.

## Rule for creating a new board

Do not create a new board until **all six existing boards have ≥30 pins** *and* at least three of them have a 30-day CTR ≥ 0.8%. New boards before that point dilute the algorithm signal.

## Expansion priority list (post month-2)

When the conditions above are met, add these in this order. Each entry includes its strategic justification.

Don't open all ten at once. Stagger one new board every 2–3 weeks.

## Quarterly review

Every 90 days, revisit:
- Are any boards under 30 pins? Pause new-board creation, double down on those.
- Are any boards over 200 pins with no sections? Add sections.
- Has Pinterest search demand shifted any keyword? Update board descriptions.
