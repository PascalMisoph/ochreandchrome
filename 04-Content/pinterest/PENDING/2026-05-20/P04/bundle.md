> Adire is a Yoruba indigo resist-dye textile tradition from Nigeria — primarily Abeokuta and Ibadan. Hand-drawn with cassava paste, stitched, or tied before the indigo bath. Here is how to recognise it, how to source it from makers who name their origin, and how to style it in a contemporary room without flattening its specificity. Read the full guide on Ochre & Chrome. #Adire #YorubaTextile #IndigoResist
