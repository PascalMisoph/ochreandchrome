## 3. Pin description (300–500 chars, paste verbatim)
> A raffia lamp does one thing no linen shade can — it filters light into a soft honey glow and reads as warm texture even switched off. Choose matte, undyed raffia over anything glossy or bright-dyed; keep the base simple, turned wood or unlacquered brass, and let the shade be the event. The result is warm summer light that works at the nightstand or on a console. Read the full guide on raffia, cane, and palm. #RaffiaLamp #AfrohemianDecor #SummerLighting
