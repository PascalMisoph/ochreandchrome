## 3. Pin description (300–500 chars, paste verbatim)
> The fastest way to flatten a caned piece is to paint the frame white. Cane earns its depth from contrast — pale open weave against a warm walnut, teak, or honey-oak frame. White-painted cane reads as flat-pack; warm-wood cane reads as collected. The same rule lets a caned sideboard or chair carry real visual weight while staying airy enough for summer. Read the full guide on layering raffia, cane, and palm. #CaneFurniture #AfrohemianDecor #SummerInteriors
