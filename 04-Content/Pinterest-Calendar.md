# Pinterest Calendar — 7-Day Rotation

This file holds the **evergreen weekly rhythm**. For the operational 30-day plan with 75 fully-specified pin ideas mapped to the 6 real boards, see [[30-Day-Pinterest-Content-Plan]] — that is the file the daily operator works from.

The 7-day pattern below loops 13 times = 91 days = **270 pins**. The AI operator iterates the target keyword each cycle based on the [[Blog-Plan-90d]].

Cadence target: **2–3 pins per day** (75 pins / 30 days). The 3-per-day version below is the upper bound; agents should drop to 2/day when production capacity is constrained — never below 2/day.

## Universal AI image prompt modifiers

**Static images:**
> Premium architectural digest style interior photography, warm natural light, cinematic composition, [subject], high-end editorial look, no people, clean lines, enough negative space at top/bottom for text overlay, aspect ratio 2:3.

**Collages:**
> Clean editorial product collage on a warm cream `#F7F5F0` background, minimalist soft drop shadows, premium magazine layout, aspect ratio 2:3.

**Video:**
> 6-second vertical video, slow smooth camera pan across a luxury [room type], warm natural light, high-end editorial styling, highly aesthetic.
