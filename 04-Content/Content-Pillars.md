# Content Pillars

## The two macro worlds (top-level)

Everything Ochre & Chrome publishes belongs to one of two aesthetic worlds:

1. **Neo Deco** — geometry, glamour, polish, chrome, brass, red marble, sculptural lighting, modern Art Deco.
2. **Afrohemian** — soul, texture, craft, African-inspired interiors, Adire textiles, woven baskets, warm earth tones, collected homes.

Neither world dominates. Afrohemian carries roughly equal strategic weight to Neo Deco (Pinterest search demand is rising fastest there). See [[Neo-Deco-vs-Afrohemian]] for the full relationship and [[Cultural-Care-Guidelines]] for Afrohemian framing rules.

The eight pillars below sit *underneath* those two worlds. Pillar selection drives the daily Pinterest rotation in [[Pinterest-Calendar]] and the 30-day plan in [[30-Day-Pinterest-Content-Plan]].
