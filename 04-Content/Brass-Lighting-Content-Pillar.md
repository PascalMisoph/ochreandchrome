## Voice notes

- Lead with the **finish**: "unlacquered solid brass," "antique brass," "polished brass," "blackened bronze."
- State the **rule** structurally: "32 inches above the island," "two-and-a-half times the table width for a chandelier."
- Justify the **splurge** materially: "it patinas over five years," "the diffuser is hand-blown fluted glass, not pressed."
- Forbidden: "bling," "gold," "fancy," "luxe" (use "premium," "heirloom-quality," "structural").

**Tier 1 (high ticket, high commission):** CB2 Vega and Carmilla pendants, Lulu & Georgia sculptural pendants, Lumens designer brands (Allied Maker, Visual Comfort).
**Tier 2 (mid):** Anthropologie brass fixtures, West Elm brass pendants.
**Tier 3 (dupes / look-for-less):** Wayfair, Amazon (low-ticket only).

Every brass pin should link to a roundup or single-fixture post — never a pin without a destination. This is the affiliate revenue board.

## Forbidden

- Cheap brass-plated steel pretending to be solid.
- Industrial Edison-bulb cliché.
- Rose gold (it's not brass).
- Generic 3D-rendered fixtures with implausible patina.
- "Bling" or "gold" copy.
