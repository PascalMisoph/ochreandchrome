# Content Pillar — Neo Deco

One of the two macro worlds. See [[Neo-Deco-vs-Afrohemian]] for the relationship and [[Neo-Deco-Visual-Guide]] for visual rules.

## Strategic role

Neo Deco is the **high-AOV, premium-affiliate** half of the brand. CB2, Lulu & Georgia, Anthropologie, West Elm, Lumens. Higher commission ceiling, longer consideration cycle, hero category for "investment piece" content.

- **Neo Deco Living Room Design** *(primary)*

- **2026 Home Decor Trends** *(when framed as a trend piece)*

## Voice notes specific to Neo Deco

- Lead with the **material**: "unlacquered solid brass," "red Portoro marble," "dense contract-grade velvet."
- Lead with the **rule**: "Hang at 32 inches above the island." "Anchor the front legs of your seating."
- Permission-to-spend framing: "Invest in the sofa. Save on the lamp." Justify the splurge structurally.
- Avoid period costume language ("flapper," "speakeasy," "1920s glamour"). Frame as *modern Art Deco*.

## Cross-pillar bridges

- **Neo Deco × Afrohemian fusion:** A red marble vanity (Neo Deco) styled with an indigo Adire-inspired hand towel (Afrohemian). Use sparingly — 1 in 5 fusion pins.
- **Neo Deco × Trend Translation:** "Why Warm Metals Are Replacing Cool Chrome" type pins live in **2026 Home Decor Trends** primarily, **Neo Deco Living Room Design** secondary.
