## The Single Highest-Leverage Purchase in a Kitchen

The brass pendant is the single highest-leverage purchase in a Neo Deco kitchen. A new pendant changes the colour temperature of every other surface in the room. Cabinets read warmer. ## What Makes a Good Neo Deco Pendant

Three criteria. Every pendant in this roundup passes all three.

**Material.** Solid brass over plated. Unlacquered brass (which patinas over time) over high-gloss lacquered brass. Look for ribbed or fluted glass shades, never frosted plastic.

**Scale.** For a kitchen island, pendant diameter should sit at roughly one-third the island's width per fixture, with two or three fixtures spaced 24 to 30 inches apart. Lumens publishes the same guideline; it is industry standard.

**Lumens output.** Task lighting over a kitchen island wants 800 to 1,100 lumens per fixture. A pretty pendant that throws only 300 lumens leaves you cooking in shadow.

## Mistakes to Avoid

**Hanging too high.** Pendants over a kitchen island should sit 30 to 36 inches above the counter, not the floor. Most homeowners hang them too high and lose the focal effect.

**Choosing form over output.** A 300-lumen sculptural pendant is a decorative object, not task lighting. If you actually cook, prioritise lumens.

**Mixing finishes inside the fixture.** Brass pendants with chrome or nickel cable canopies look unresolved. Match the canopy to the body.

**Forgetting the bulb.** Use 2700K warm-white LEDs at minimum. A daylight 5000K bulb in a brass pendant cancels the warmth that justified the brass.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How many pendants over a kitchen island?**
Two for islands under 6 feet, three for islands 6 to 9 feet, one linear fixture or larger cluster for 9 feet and up.

**What is the difference between unlacquered and lacquered brass?**
Lacquered brass has a clear coat that prevents tarnishing; the finish stays uniform but never develops patina. Unlacquered brass ages and patinas over years; this is what designers specify in editorial kitchens.

**Will brass go out of style?**
Brass cycles, but warm brass has had a sustained run since 2018, and Neo Deco entrenches it through 2026 and beyond. Polished chrome is the alternative finish if you want to hedge.

**Is unlacquered brass hard to maintain in a kitchen?**
The opposite. Unlacquered brass is forgiving because every fingerprint and water spot becomes part of the patina. Lacquered brass is harder; once the lacquer scratches, the finish fails unevenly.
