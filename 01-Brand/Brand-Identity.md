# Brand Identity

## Final Brand System

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Brand name** | Ochre & Chrome |
| **Concept** | The digital intersection of global heritage and modernized glamour |
| **Positioning** | We curate high-end, culturally rich, and architecturally bold interior design aesthetics for the modern homeowner |
| **Headline / brand line** | **Design With Depth.** *(Primary — used on the website, hero, lead magnet, and brand-led pins.)* |
| **Subline** | Curated aesthetics, shoppable guides, and styling rules for the intentional modern home. |
| **Secondary tagline** | Intentional living. Curated spaces. *(Used in profile bios, footers, sign-offs.)* |
| **Mission** | Help consumers escape sterile minimalism by sourcing pieces that offer texture, history, and striking visual impact |
| **Promise** | Every product recommendation elevates the home's aesthetic value |
| **Archetype** | The Curator / The Creator |
| **Two core aesthetic worlds** | **Neo Deco** (geometry, glamour, polish) + **Afrohemian** (soul, texture, craft). See [[Neo-Deco-vs-Afrohemian]]. |

## Audience

**Primary:** Gen X and Millennials, high household income, currently renovating or redecorating, fatigued by fast furniture.

- **Emotional promise:** Your home will feel like an expensive, thoughtfully curated boutique hotel.
- **Commercial promise:** We find the exact pieces you need to execute high-end trends without the $10,000 interior designer fee.

## What the brand IS

Editorial · Bold · Worldly · Polished · Authoritative

## What the brand is NOT

Cheap · Sterile · Generic · Farmhouse · Overly DIY · Cluttered

## Name selection rationale

Chosen from a 20-option longlist. **Ochre & Chrome** wins because it physically describes the two trends (earthy *ochre* = Afrohemian, *chrome* = Neo Deco), is premium and memorable, and reads like a high-end boutique interior design firm.

Domain: `ochreandchrome.com` · Handle: `@ochreandchrome`
