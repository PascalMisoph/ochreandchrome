## Selection Checklist

Before adding a product to a roundup, every box must be ticked:

- [ ] **Visual Fit** — Does it look expensive? Retailer photography is high-res with a white background available?
- [ ] **Aesthetic Match** — Explicitly Neo Deco (geometric/metal) or Afrohemian (textural/cultural)?
- [ ] **Stock Availability** — Core collection item, not clearance? (Clearance items break.)
- [ ] **Reviews** — At least 4.0 rating?
- [ ] **Commission Profile** — Pays ≥5%, OR AOV high enough to justify a lower rate?
- [ ] **Brand Trust** — Will the user feel secure entering their credit card on the destination site?
