# Website Architecture

## Identity

| | |
|---|---|
| Site name | Ochre & Chrome |
| Domain | `ochreandchrome.com` |
| Homepage H1 | **Design With Depth.** |
| Subheadline | Curated aesthetics, shoppable guides, and styling rules for the intentional modern home. |

### Homepage intro
> Welcome to Ochre & Chrome, your digital curator for 2026's most compelling interior design movements. We bridge the gap between architectural Neo Deco glamour and soulful Afrohemian warmth. Discover expert styling guides, avoid costly design mistakes, and shop our rigorously vetted furniture and decor edits.

### About page
> We believe homes should not look like sterile showrooms. Ochre & Chrome was founded to help the modern homeowner navigate the shift away from fast-furniture minimalism. We spend hundreds of hours analyzing trends, testing materials, and sourcing the finest affiliate products — from authentic African artisans on Etsy to high-end sculptural lighting on CB2 — so you can curate a space that tells your story.

### Footer description
> Ochre & Chrome is an independent interior design publisher and curation engine. We help you build a home with soul, structure, and style.

### Affiliate disclosure
> Ochre & Chrome is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we rigorously evaluate for aesthetic and structural quality.

### Newsletter
- **Headline:** The Curator's Edit.
- **Description:** Join 10,000+ design enthusiasts. Get our weekly breakdown of emerging decor trends, exclusive discount codes, and shoppable room blueprints sent straight to your inbox.

### Lead magnet
- **Title:** The 2026 Room Reset Blueprint
- **Description:** Stop guessing. Get the exact paint colors, spatial rules, and shoppable links to effortlessly blend Neo Deco glamour with Afrohemian warmth.
- **Format:** PDF cheat sheet — paint hex codes, metal finish pairing rules, clickable shopping list.

### Homepage section structure
1. Hero with Lead Magnet opt-in
2. Shop by Aesthetic (split: Neo Deco / Afrohemian)
3. Latest Styling Guides (3-grid)
4. The Curated Cart (4-grid affiliate roundups)
5. Trend Report (link to macro article)
6. Footer
