# Brass & Statement Lighting — Visual Guide

A specialist sub-guide for the **Statement Brass Pendant Lighting** board and all brass-led pin content. Lighting is the single highest-AOV affiliate category — the visuals must do real commercial work.

## Mood in one sentence

A single brass pendant is doing the job of three lesser fixtures, and you can see the patina on it.

## What good brass looks like

- **Unlacquered solid brass.** Develops a living patina over years. Warm, slightly variable colour. The premium tier.
- **Antique brass / aged brass.** Pre-darkened finish, hides fingerprints, reads vintage.
- **Polished brass.** Mirror-bright, reads more contemporary, scratches faster.
- **Blackened bronze / oil-rubbed bronze.** A Neo Deco contrast metal.

What good brass is **not**: thin brass-plated steel that chips on the edge, plastic shades sprayed gold, big-box "antique gold" that reads yellow.

## Composition rules for pins

| Pin type | Layout |
|---|---|
| Hero pendant | Single fixture, 60% of frame, warm light pool below, marble or walnut surface receiving the light |
| Three-pendant row | Over a kitchen island in red marble or warm walnut, even spacing, side angle to show the sculpture |
| Wall sconce flank | Two sconces flanking an arched mirror or a piece of art, symmetric composition |
| Floor lamp scene | Sculptural brass floor lamp beside velvet armchair, side table with fluted glass, warm shadow on the wall |
| Affiliate roundup grid | 4 fixtures, transparent backgrounds on cream `#F7F5F0`, soft drop shadow, prices visible |

## Lighting the lighting

- The light *source* is the subject. The pin should make you want to turn it on.
- Show a warm pool on the surface beneath. Never floodlight the whole room — that defeats the point.
- Photograph at dusk or with strong directional warm key light. Avoid daylight overhead.

## Materials it pairs with (for the pin scene)

- Red Portoro or Calacatta Viola marble surface
- Dark walnut or burl wood
- Fluted glass diffuser or fluted glassware nearby
- Velvet seating in jewel tone (Neo Deco) **or** linen + jute table styling (Afrohemian fusion)
- Matte-black hardware (counterpoint metal)

## Forbidden

- Cheap thin brass-plate, IKEA-feel symmetry, identical big-box pendants in a row.
- Industrial-loft Edison-bulb cliché.
- Rose gold (it's not brass).
- Generic 3D-rendered pendants — the patina has to read real.
