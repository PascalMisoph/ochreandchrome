# Neo Deco — Visual Guide

For pin imagery, room photography, Midjourney prompts, and Canva backgrounds in the **Neo Deco** world. Pair with [[Visual-Direction]] (universal rules), [[Color-Palette]] (hex), and [[Neo-Deco-vs-Afrohemian]] (when to choose this world).

## Mood in one sentence

A boutique-hotel lounge after-hours: brass pools of light, a fluted glass on red marble, velvet absorbing the shadow.

## Palette

| Role | Color | Hex / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Walls / background | Warm ivory · deep oxblood · charcoal · lacquered black | `#F7F5F0`, `#3A1416`, `#2C2C2C` |
| Hero metal | Unlacquered solid brass | warm yellow, slight patina |
| Counter metal | Polished chrome | mirror finish, restraint |
| Stone | Red marble (Portoro, Levanto, Calacatta Viola) | dramatic veining |
| Wood | Lacquered black, dark walnut, burl | high-sheen |
| Velvet | Emerald, oxblood, sapphire, ochre | dense pile, light-absorbent |
| Accent | Brushed brass `#D4AF37` only as small UI accent — never as fill |

## Materials checklist (a Neo Deco pin should have ≥3 of these)

- Unlacquered solid brass (lighting, hardware, frame)
- Fluted or ribbed glass (vase, sconce, glassware)
- Red or moody marble (surface, tray, plinth)
- Velvet (sofa, chair, headboard, drapery)
- Lacquered or burl wood (cabinet, console, coffee table)
- Geometric mirror (fan, stepped, rounded-rectangle)
- Chevron or parquet flooring

## Geometry signatures

- **Fan arches** — over doorways, in mirror shape, in tile pattern.
- **Chevron** — flooring, upholstery, decorative tile.
- **Stepped silhouettes** — bar carts, mirrors, console legs.
- **Vertical fluting / ribbing** — glass, wood, plaster.
- **Sunburst / radial** — mirror, light fixture.
- **Gold-section symmetry** — pendant pairs, sconce flanks.

## Composition

- Cinematic. Warm key light, soft shadow on the right.
- Hero object lives slightly off-centre, framed by negative space.
- One focal pool of light per shot. Never spotlit-everything.
- Surface reflections allowed and encouraged on marble and lacquer.

## Forbidden

- Vegas-Deco gold-plate kitsch.
- 1980s pastel pastiche.
- Plastic shades pretending to be brass.
- "Roaring 20s" costume props (feathers, cocktail menus on screen).
- Cluttered "more is more" maximalism — Neo Deco is *restrained* glamour.

## Midjourney prompt formula

> Premium architectural digest interior photography, [room type] in Neo Deco style, warm natural light from camera left, [hero object: e.g. unlacquered brass pendant], red marble surface, fluted glass detail, jewel-tone velvet [furniture], lacquered black or dark walnut wood, fan-arch mirror, cinematic restraint, no people, 2:3 aspect ratio, generous negative space at top and bottom for text overlay, editorial magazine composition.

## Sample on-pin overlay copy

- *"The Neo Deco Revival."*
- *"Brass + Marble Done Right."*
- *"7 Brass Pendants Under $300."*
- *"Red Marble Is Replacing White Quartz."*

## Reference rooms

- AD penthouse features (warm-toned, brass-led)
- Boutique cocktail bars (Aman New York, The NoMad)
- 1930s ocean-liner lounges, modernised
