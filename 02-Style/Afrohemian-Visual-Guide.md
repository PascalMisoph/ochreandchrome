# Afrohemian — Visual Guide

For pin imagery, room photography, Midjourney prompts, and Canva backgrounds in the **Afrohemian** world. Pair with [[Cultural-Care-Guidelines]] (mandatory), [[Visual-Direction]], [[Color-Palette]], and [[Neo-Deco-vs-Afrohemian]].

## Mood in one sentence

A traveller's home in late-afternoon light: layered textiles on a low sofa, a clay vessel on a carved-wood stool, the air still warm.

## Palette

| Role | Color | Hex / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Walls / background | Warm ivory · sand · clay plaster · deep ochre · tobacco | `#F7F5F0`, `#E5D7BC`, `#C48B36`, `#A95C45` |
| Earth tones | Terracotta · rust · clay · walnut · burnt sienna | hand-formed feeling |
| Accent | Indigo (Adire-inspired) · oxidised brass · matte black | sparingly |
| Naturals | Linen ivory · jute brown · sisal cream · raw cotton | dominant fibre |
| Forbidden | Cool greys · neon · pastel mandala · saturated "boho" rainbow |

## Materials checklist (an Afrohemian pin should have ≥3 of these)

- Layered textiles (linen + jute + wool + indigo cotton)
- Hand-loomed throw or pillow (Adire-inspired, mudcloth-inspired, kuba-inspired — see cultural rules)
- Woven natural-fibre rug (jute, sisal, hand-knotted Moroccan-style)
- Carved wood stool, side table, or bench
- Ceramic vessel (terracotta, clay, raw earthenware)
- Woven basket cluster (Tonga / Bolga / Senegalese — credit when known)
- Dried botanical (pampas, palm leaf, branches) — sparingly
- Oxidised or aged brass accent (lamp, candlestick, frame)

## Geometry signatures

- **Irregular hand-formed shapes** — pinch-pot ceramics, hand-thrown vessels.
- **Concentric weave patterns** — basket walls.
- **Indigo resist-dye irregularity** — Adire-inspired textiles.
- **Low horizontal lines** — poufs, low sofas, tatami-height surfaces.
- **Curved organic forms** — carved stools, gourd lamps.

## Composition

- Warm afternoon light, side-raking. Soft long shadows.
- Eye-level or slightly low — the room is *lived in*, not photographed from above.
- Layered foreground / mid / background. Never empty.
- Visible texture is the point — close-crops on weave, ceramic surface, indigo resist edge are all on-brand.

## Forbidden

- "Tribal" anything in the visual or copy. See [[Cultural-Care-Guidelines]].
- Dreamcatchers, mandala wall hangings, generic "African mask" décor.
- Pastel boho rainbow palettes.
- Beige-on-beige minimalism marketed as "warm" — Afrohemian is *layered*, not bland.
- AI-generated images claiming authenticity. Caption as inspired.
- Generic Moroccan-themed AirBnB clichés (lanterns hanging in clusters, etc.).

## Midjourney prompt formula

> Premium architectural digest interior photography, [room type] in Afrohemian style, warm late-afternoon natural light from camera left, layered linen and jute textiles, [hero textile: Adire-inspired indigo cushion / hand-loomed throw], terracotta ceramic vessel, carved wood stool, woven basket wall cluster, ochre and tobacco palette, oxidised brass candlestick accent, irregular hand-formed shapes, no people, 2:3 aspect ratio, generous negative space at top and bottom for text overlay, magazine editorial composition, lived-in warmth.
